The Enterprise AI Hypothesis

What if Business Processes Become the Primary Artifact?

A hypothesis that AI should begin with business processes—not tables, APIs or semantic models.

The Problem

Enterprise AI has access to data but often lacks business context. Knowledge is scattered across documentation, APIs, semantic models and prompts.

The Hypothesis

Business processes may become the primary artifact for enterprise AI. Humans describe intent, process, business concepts, governance and systems. AI consumes that context to orchestrate work.
